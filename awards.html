Global Student Entrepreneur Awards 2020

National finalist

Team Entrepreneurs' Organization and GSEA Sri Lanka, you people have done a wonderful job on organizing the Global Student Entrepreneur Awards this year in Sri Lanka. It was a wonderful and different experience that I had in the past four-year hackathon/ competition journey. It was my pleasure to get to know these outstanding student entrepreneurs as a digital intrapreneur. Congratulations to the other three finalists and the national champion. I am not either a founder or a co-founder of an active business but still was able to make it to the Global Student Entrepreneur Awards 2020 as a National finalist along with the founders of million-rupee worth businesses. This opportunity wouldn’t have been possible without the support given by my beloved institution Informatics Institute of Technology (IIT) and Cookoo Eats (Pvt) Ltd., the startup which I represented. Personally, I believe that this is not just a competition but an opportunity I received to network and get to know the future leaders of our Lankan startup ecosystem. Keep exploring!

E-Swabhimani Awards 2019 - Business & e-Commerce

Youth Awards

We are so thankful for Information and Communication Technology Agency of Sri Lanka (ICTA) for recognizing our effort at E-Swabhimani Gala Awards 2019 and for awarding Cookoo Eats at Digital Social Impact Awards as one of the Winners under the Business & e-Commerce Youth category. (The e-Swabhimani is an initiative of the Information Communication Technology Agency of Sri Lanka aimed at recognizing excellence in digital application creation) A business’s strategy cannot be achieved without the team, at this prestigious moment on behalf of the team as a digital innovator & intrapreneur, I would like to appreciate the tremendous support and contribution given by our teammates, from the interns to the delivery heroes who have worked and working harder behind the success of Jaffna’s first-ever homemade food delivery startup Cookoo eats (Pvt) Ltd.. Happy to be a part of this energetic team. Together we will acquire knowledge and shall make this world better!

IEEE Awards 2019 - Best Student Branch Activity

Code Sprint Startup Battle 3.0

CodeSprint the premier Inter-University Hackathon of Sri Lanka organized by the IEEE Student Branch of Informatics Institute of Technology, partnering up with Dialog Ideamart. CodeSprint organized consecutively in the IEEE year calendar . This year CodeSprint 3.0 taking on the theme of “Youth Entrepreneurship Incubator and Startup Battle”, not only gives a platform for undergraduates for ideation, but also a platform for entrepreneurial and intrapreneurial endeavors.CodeSprint will take the participants on a journey from ideation to MVP through workshops and mentorship. Undergraduates and digital innovators will be given a span of one month to hack on their tech-based ideas, market-validate it and then put forward their MVP to a panel of investors to present their startup idea. As the co-chair person of the initative I had the privilege to Head the sponsor committee to find companies on board and also I too contributed in planning and executing the event.

Innovation Nation Sri Lanka 2019 - IEEE Sri Lanka Section

Second Runner up

For every idea that needs a platform to showcase its worth, IEEE Innovation Nation 2018 is the competition for it. IEEE being the world's largest professional society spearheading standards and most of the research literature in the world; partnering with IEEE provide ample opportunities to provide the young minds and digital innovators with opportunities to transform them into successful entrepreneurs and intrapreneurs through this competition. IEEE Innovation Nation will provide training, mentoring, financial support and most importantly expose the global market to budding entrepreneurs and intrapreneurs to take their idea to the next level. At the end of the rigorous program, we aim to add at least fifty young people to the Sri Lankan workforce every year who are fully trained in all related areas to start a successful business.
